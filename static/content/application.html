Bridging the gap with model robustness

Some text

The Wavelet Attribution Method (WAM) is a novel approach to feature attribution that leverages the wavelet transform to provide a unified perspective on model interpretability across various modalities, including images, audio, and 3D volumes. By decomposing the model's decision-making process in the wavelet domain, WAM offers deeper insights into the model's behavior, revealing not only where it focuses but also how it interprets structural information in the input data.
